Xue Fangli stared at his lips and drank the Autumn Dew White from the pot. Jiang Juan, after a while, vaguely remembered something, and shook his arm, "Young Lord, leave me some."

Xue Fangli said with regret, "It seems to be finished."

Jiang Juan frowned, let out an "ah," and asked, "Finished?"

This time, when Jiang Juan tried to take the pot again, Xue Fangli released his grip. Jiang Juan shook a few times, really finishing it off, and he said sadly, "There's not a drop left."

Xue Fangli smiled, held Jiang Juan's chin, lifted his head, and slowly said, "There's still one drop."

Jiang Juan opened his eyes slowly, "Where is it?"

Xue Fangli looked at him with a smile, his lips red with a hint of wine. His voice was soft and slow, as if he were whispering to entice, "Find it yourself."

"I can't find it."

Jiang Juan shook the pot again; there really wasn't any left. He could only lift his head blankly, only to have his finger suddenly grabbed and lifted, placed on those red lips.

Xue Fangli lowered his eyes, "Do you want to drink?"
