Xue Congyun and Jiang Qingliang felt somewhat guilty, but Jiang Qingliang still defended himself, "Isn't it about giving Niege his due? Didn't we see him last time too? He was so weak that he nearly fainted from anger. Can our conscience be at ease with that?"

Gu Puwang said coolly, "I just thought that Niege has always been kind-hearted. Even if we give him satisfaction, and the person gets seriously hurt, knowing this, Niege would definitely feel guilty."

"Rebuking is too harsh. Why don't we have a nice chat about this matter? Ask him what his thoughts are on Niege."

Xue Congyun: "Fine with me."

Jiang Qingliang: "Okay."

The three of them reached an agreement, and Gu Puwang said, "I'll count to three, and then we all go in together."

Xue Congyun interjected: "Alright, the three of us will advance and retreat together. Whoever runs away from battle is a coward."

Jiang Qingliang put away her laughter, "Problem."

Gu Puwang nodded seriously, "Mm."

"Three, two, one."

The next second, the tent flap was lifted.

Jiang Juan, who was drinking water, froze upon seeing Xue Congyun rush in.

Here they come.

The main characters have come to cause trouble for him.

He knew it all along.

Jiang Juan's heart was pounding with anxiety as he stared unwaveringly at Xue Congyun.

Xue Congyun: "Brother Nie last night——"

As soon as he spoke a few words, something felt off. Xue Congyun looked left and right, finally realizing the issue. He gritted his teeth and said through clenched jaws, "Jiang Qingliang, Gu Puwang, are you two even human beings!"

Alright, advance and retreat together, but it turns out only Xue Congyun took it seriously. Jiang Qingliang and Gu Puwang these two beasts didn't even enter the tent.

And just as he finished speaking, two voices came from outside the tent.

Gu Puwang: "Woof."

Jiang Qingliang: "Woof woof woof."

Xue Congyun: "???"

This time, he truly had his heart set on it.

Xue Congyun fell into silence. Jiang Juan could only ask him proactively, "What? What do you want?"

Xue Congyun: "..."

Don't panic; stay calm. How did that just happen?

Brother Juan is frail and can't be scolded or criticized. I should have a good chat with Brother Juan about what happened last night and find out his thoughts on Niange.

Xue Congyun calmed his mind and spoke with confidence, "Brother Juan, do you know the four-eared cat? Among all cats with two ears, only those in Jianzhou Prefecture of Sichuan have overlapping contours, forming two large and two small ears, making a total of four ears."

"A few days ago, His Majesty the Emperor got a four-eared cat. It's quite adorable. I have a horse-riding competition soon, and if I win, I can take this cat home to keep it. Do you want to keep it? I'll bring it back for you to play with."

Jiang Qingliang: "..."

Gu Puwang: "..."

They were speechless, and Jiang Juan was also quite surprised.

Jiang Juan: "???"

What happened?

Given that he's the main character in this as the Sixth Prince, shouldn't he be severely scolded instead?

Why is he even getting a cat?

Jiang Juan was utterly confused.

Isn't this Sixth Prince actually a fool?
