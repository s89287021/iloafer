Jiang Juan was puzzled, "Ah?"

Xue Fangli lowered his eyelids, "Born delicate and with a big temper; the slightest inconvenience would make him extend his claws to scratch this king. But when he's good, he really is obedient, able to lie in this king's arms for an entire day."
